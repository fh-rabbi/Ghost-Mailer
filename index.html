Generate App Password In Your Gmail Account

  1. Sign in to your gmail account
  2. Go to manage your account
  3. Click on the security tab
  4. Scroll down and click on 2-step-verification and turn on it
  5. After enabled 2-step-verification go to again security tab and Scroll-Down and you will see App Password click on this and generate app password
  6. Copy this password and use this password for less secure app.

    7. Less Sucure App: Non google app,like Gmail-Bombing tool etc.

Now you can use this password in Gmail-Bombing tool
